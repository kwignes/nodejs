About me

I just completed the fast-paced Technical Web Design Program at BCIT and now ready to take on various web projects. My specialization is Front-end but I love learning elements of the Back-End such as Node.js, Ruby and Python. BCIT's program provided me with the modern skills of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP and WordPress to develop professional websites that are both usable and adaptive. I received hands-on experience working with a client in a five week time span to develop a website using WordPress Underscores theme. It was fully customizable with a limited amount of plugins to provide a quality, easy to manage website.

Prior to BCIT, I received my Business degree from the University of Maine at Machias and played soccer for the Men's Varsity team. I really enjoy a team atmosphere whether it be developing a site or on the soccer pitch.
