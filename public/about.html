About me

I just completed the fast-paced Technical Web Design Program at BCIT and now ready to take on various web projects. My specialization is Front-End but I love learning elements of the Back-End such as Node, Ruby and Python. BCIT's program provided me with the modern skills of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP and WordPress to develop professional, adaptive websites. I received hands-on experience working with a client in a five week time span to develop a website with WordPress Underscores theme. It was made fully customizable for the client and included a limited amount of plugins providing a quality, manageable website.

Prior to BCIT, I received my Business degree from the University of Maine at Machias and played soccer for the Men's Varsity team. In my spare time, I like to learn new technologies, hike the North Shore mountains, play board games, and help others with tech related problems through my YouTube channel.
